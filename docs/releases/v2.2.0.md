# portalfin v2.2.0

Adds support for the **Immortal** community launcher.

[Immortal](https://github.com/) is a community-built launcher that revives old
Facebook Portals. It replaces the Portal's OEM launcher and — unlike the stock
OS — does **not** draw the system OSD band (the floating back/home buttons at the
top-left). Without those, portalfin had no back or home affordance when running
under Immortal. This release detects Immortal and draws its own.

## What's new

### Immortal launcher navigation
- **Auto-detected.** When the Immortal launcher is your home, portalfin shows its
  own back & home buttons in the top-left — styled to match the OEM Portal OSD
  pills (translucent dark stadium pills, white icons) as closely as possible.
- **Back** pops the current screen (normal back behavior).
- **Home** drops you back out to the Immortal launcher — mirroring the OEM home
  button.
- On the stock Portal OS nothing changes: the OEM OSD still handles back/home and
  portalfin stays out of the way.
- **Manual override** in Profile → Navigation: **Auto** (default; detect Immortal),
  **On** (always show portalfin's buttons), or **Off** (never).

## Sideload

The download link always points at the newest release:

1. `adb devices` confirms your Portal is connected (ADB enabled in Settings → Debug)
2. Download **[portalfin.apk](https://github.com/luke-hurd/portalfin/releases/latest/download/portalfin.apk)**
3. `adb install -r portalfin.apk` (the `-r` upgrades in place)
