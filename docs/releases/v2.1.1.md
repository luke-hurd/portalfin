# portalfin v2.1.1

A small player-polish release.

## Fixes

- **No more stray brightness/volume overlay.** Tapping the video to pause or
  toggle fullscreen could intermittently flash the brightness or volume gesture
  overlay — a near-vertical micro-movement in the tap was being read as a swipe.
  The brightness/volume swipe gestures are now off by default (the Portal is a
  fixed touchscreen, where swipe-to-adjust isn't intuitive). Tap-to-pause,
  double-tap-to-seek, and pinch-to-zoom are unchanged.
- **Bright picture every time.** The player now forces the screen to full
  brightness while a video plays (restored to the system brightness on exit), so
  playback is never dim regardless of the Portal's ambient brightness.

## Sideload

The download link always points at the newest release:

1. `adb devices` confirms your Portal is connected (ADB enabled in Settings → Debug)
2. Download **[portalfin.apk](https://github.com/luke-hurd/portalfin/releases/latest/download/portalfin.apk)**
3. `adb install -r portalfin.apk` (the `-r` upgrades in place)
