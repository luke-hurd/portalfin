# portalfin v2.2.1

Polish for the Immortal launcher support added in v2.2.0, plus an ambient tweak.

## Changes

- **Immortal nav is now fully automatic.** Removed the Auto/On/Off setting —
  portalfin just detects the launcher and shows the right back/home buttons. The
  detection is more robust (correctly ignores Immortal when it's installed but not
  the active launcher).
- **Custom back/home buttons refined** — wider, translucent-white capsules that
  better match the Portal's own OSD, with a touch more spacing from the edge and
  between the two buttons.
- **Ambient screensaver legibility** — the clock, date, and title art now sit on
  top of the bottom gradient instead of being dimmed underneath it.
- portalfin never touches the system navigation bar during normal browsing (a
  regression fix): the Portal's own back/home OSD is left entirely to the OS on
  the stock launcher.

> Note: the Immortal launcher force-hides the Portal's system back/home OSD
> globally (an OS setting it applies on install). That's why portalfin's own
> buttons are needed under Immortal — and why nothing changes on the stock OS.

## Sideload

The download link always points at the newest release:

1. `adb devices` confirms your Portal is connected (ADB enabled in Settings → Debug)
2. Download **[portalfin.apk](https://github.com/luke-hurd/portalfin/releases/latest/download/portalfin.apk)**
3. `adb install -r portalfin.apk` (the `-r` upgrades in place)
